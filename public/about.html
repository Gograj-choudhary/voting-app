About

Welcome to our Voting App! This is a platform where you can conduct voting events or participate by casting your vote for candidates. You can use this app in schools, colleges, or communities for secure and easy voting.

1. How to Use It

  • Become an Admin: If you want to conduct voting, choose the **Admin** role during sign-up.
  • Become a Voter: If you want to cast your vote, choose the **Voter** role during sign-up.
  • Sign Up: Provide the required information and complete the registration process.

2. Creating a Room (For Admins)

  • As an admin, after signing up, you will have the option to create a voting room.
  • You need to set a **Room Name** and a **Room ID**.
  • Share the **Room ID** with your voters so they can access the candidates for voting.
  • If you have already created a room, you can log in using the **Room ID**.

3. Adding Candidates

Once the room is created, you will see an **"Add Candidates"** option.

Here, you can add candidates by entering their details.

After adding all candidates, click on **"Submit"** to finalize the candidate list.

4. Deleting a Candidate

  • If you need to remove a candidate after submitting, you can use this option.
  • Enter the candidate's **Aadhar Card Number** and delete the entry.

5. Updating Candidate Information

  • If you need to update candidate details, use this option.
  • Enter the candidate's **Aadhar Card Number** and click **"Find"** to retrieve their information.
  • Update the details and click **"Update"** to save the changes.

6. Viewing Vote Counts

  • Here, you can see the total votes received by each candidate.
  • Candidates are listed in **descending order**, from the highest to the lowest votes.
  • Admins can **publish** the results for voters once the voting process is completed.

7. Logging into a Room (For Voters)

  • To cast a vote, you must **log in using the Room ID** provided by the admin.
  • Enter the Room ID and access the voting interface.

8. Voting for Candidates

  • After logging into a room, you will see a list of all candidates.
  • You can view candidate profiles and cast your vote by clicking the **"Vote"** button.
  • You can vote **only once** and for **only one candidate** in a particular room.

9. Viewing Voting Results

  • After casting your vote, you will be able to see the **voting results**.
  • The results will be visible **only after voting ends** and the admin **publishes the results**.

Thank You!
